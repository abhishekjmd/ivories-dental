Common Concerns

Addressing your questions about Root Canal Treatment

Does the procedure really not hurt?
expand_more
Yes. Modern root canal treatment is very similar to having a routine filling. We use localized anesthesia and pain-management protocols that ensure you are completely comfortable throughout the visit.
How long does the appointment take?
expand_more
A typical microscopic rotary RCT at Ivories takes between 45 to 90 minutes. In most cases, the entire procedure can be completed in a single appointment.
Is a crown always necessary after an RCT?
expand_more
Usually, yes. Since the tooth loses its blood supply after an RCT, it becomes more brittle. A dental crown protects the tooth from fractures and restores its full chewing function.
How do I know if I need a Root Canal?
expand_more
Common signs include severe pain while chewing, sensitivity to hot or cold that lingers, tooth discoloration, or swelling in the gums. A digital X-ray at our clinic will confirm the diagnosis.
